Update 1.0.0!

1.0.0 is the first release ever! HYPE!!!!

Here's a short list of what we've done:

  • Actual real content. We've imported our work from a previous repository, due to some security concerns regarding personal information. That's why there is so much content in 1.0.0! I'll briefly summarize what we did before the creation of this repository:

    • a. Login system. Google and GitHub oAuth logins are functional, as well as standard email login.
    • b. Firebase. Your data syncs to our database, allowing you to access all of your preferences from any device! Don't worry, we do not collect any personal information- just your email and ElectroTab settings (dashboard layout, colors, etc.)
    • c. Drag and Drop grid. We're creating more modules right now, but we created the Logo, Search Bar, and Clock.

  • New widget- the speed dial! This widget is a bookmark for your favorite links. It displays a screenshot of the website it links to.

  • New improved database linking! More efficient and more secure. This should prevent against database leaks (another reason why we had to create a new repo).

  • Awesome home page! Before, we just had an "under construction" holding page. Now, it's actually looking pretty clean!
